# Fresh Web Research: Germany Salon + Handwerk WhatsApp Bots (March 2026)

## German salon market
- 80,500-90,000 salons in Germany
- ~55,000 small/medium/large salons; 2,500 first-class (5+ employees); ~30,000 micro-salons
- ~145,000 salaried hairdressers (March 2025)
- Trend: downsizing toward smaller teams, more part-time, flexible mini-salons

### Salon gap: same as UAE - no WhatsApp-native conversational booking
- Shore sends reminders via WhatsApp but booking is web-based
- Treatwell charges up to 35% commission on new customer bookings
- Planity is app/web-based
- n8n templates exist but require developer setup (not turnkey for salon owners)

## German Handwerk market (Bitkom Studie 2025, 504 businesses surveyed)
- 1,038,254 registered businesses; 5.6M employees
- Revenue 2024: EUR 770.7B
- 250,000+ unfilled positions; 20,000+ unfilled apprenticeship slots
- 62% already use WhatsApp/Telegram for communication
- Only 4% use AI; 9% plan to adopt soon
- Only 29% have AI-capable employees
- 72% say "too busy" for digitalization
- Self-rated digitalization: school grade 3.0 (mediocre, unchanged since 2022)

### Handwerk opportunity: massive but Germany-only
- 4,000 meiti.ai users out of 1,038,254 businesses = 0.4% penetration

- EUR 42-119/month price range fits the market
- 20+ hours time savings monthly per business (meiti.ai data)
