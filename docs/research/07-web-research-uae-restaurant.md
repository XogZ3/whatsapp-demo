## Talabat commission rates (verified 2025-2026)
- Standard: 15-35% depending on tier
- UAE specifically: 20-30% range
- Effective rate on AED 100 order: 32.5% (commission + processing + delivery)
- Restaurant net on AED 100 order: AED 67.50
- 60% of UAE restaurants rely on Talabat as primary delivery platform
- 17,500 operating restaurants in UAE
- Home delivery = 35% of restaurant business, heading to 40%

## UAE restaurant tech adoption (SevenRooms survey, April 2025)
- 87% of UAE restaurant owners already using AI
- 95% of UAE consumers comfortable with AI-driven reservations
- Only 42% have adopted AI for processing bookings (adoption gap)

## Market size
- UAE full-service restaurants: $9.54B (2025) -> $25.97B by 2031 (18.19% CAGR)
- UAE online food delivery: $720.7M (2024) -> $1,799.1M by 2033
