## The one real competitor: Happoin
- Free plan + paid from $13/month
- HappiBot 2.0: AI WhatsApp chatbot for booking/cancellation/rescheduling
- 24/7 availability
- WhatsApp reminders (35% no-show reduction claimed)
- Google Calendar + Stripe integration
- **Gaps**: No POS, no inventory, no staff scheduling, no loyalty program, no multi-language, small company with limited market presence

## Confirmed market gaps (from web data)

1. **No full-stack WhatsApp-native salon platform**: Happoin is closest but lacks core salon features (POS, staff management, loyalty)
2. **Conversational AI is primitive everywhere**: most bots are rule-based decision trees
3. **In-chat payments are rare**: most send external payment links
4. **Post-booking engagement is an afterthought**: loyalty, re-engagement, review collection underserved
5. **Regional fragmentation**: India has specialized players; Europe/MENA lack equivalents
6. **Usage-based pricing is absent**: no major player aligns cost with per-booking revenue

## Key statistics (from web sources)
- WhatsApp booking drives 27% more revenue, 35% higher retention, 45% more repeat bookings
- Average business gains $1,500/month additional revenue from WhatsApp bookings
- KFC India WhatsApp: 115K orders in 6 months, 30% lower bounce, 18% higher repeats
- Automated WhatsApp reminders reduce salon no-shows by 34-50%
