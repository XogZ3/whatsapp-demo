## Lieferando commission structure (verified)
- Own delivery: 13%
- Lieferando delivery: 30%
- Payment processing: EUR 0.69 per online transaction
- EUR 25,000/mo revenue restaurant pays EUR 4,052/mo in total fees
- Fee increases: +1% across Europe citing inflation; customer service fee rose from 2.5% to 5%
- 15,000+ restaurants connected; 11M+ monthly audience; 2.4M downloads in 2023

## La Taverna case study (Tawern, Germany - official WhatsApp Business story)
- Pizzeria in town of 2,000-3,000 people
- Delivery grew ~15% after WhatsApp adoption (June 2018)
- Delivery radius expanded by 12+ miles
- Eliminated third-party delivery service fees
